OLD SCHOOL TYLER QUESTIONS

Is this a full history project?

No. Old School Tyler is a small, opinionated archive. It highlights a few tournaments, teams and logos that mattered locally and gives them a clean, machine readable home.

How does this connect to AI SEO?

People still search for old Tyler teams and events. When those entities have structured pages, AI engines can map those memories to the city, nearby venues and modern businesses.

Can more Old School Tyler items be added?

Yes. This section is designed to grow. New cards and stories can be added for other retired tournaments, venues or teams that deserve a proper archive page.
