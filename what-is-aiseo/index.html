DEFINITION

WHAT AISEO IS

AISEO is how you make your business understandable to AI systems that recommend companies. It is built for Perplexity, Copilot, DeepSeek, and AI Overviews.

AI tools do not experience your site like a customer. They extract meaning. They try to confirm facts. They decide if your business is safe to recommend.

What machines look for first

  • Who you are: business name, category, credentials, ownership signals
  • What you do: clear services, products, boundaries, and exclusions
  • Where you serve: Texas cities, neighborhoods, counties, service areas
  • Proof: reviews, policies, photos, case results, awards, real examples

If your site is the clearest source of truth, AI can confidently pull from it. If it is vague or inconsistent, AI goes shopping for a better source and that is usually your competitor.

MECHANICS

HOW AI PICKS WHAT TO SAY

When a user asks a question, AI answers are usually assembled in layers. Your visibility depends on how well your business survives extraction and verification.

  • Baseline: the model already understands categories and patterns
  • Grounding: it pulls web sources to confirm facts and context
  • Precision: it uses structured cues and clean on-site facts to lock in accuracy

AISEO makes sure your site is the easiest place to confirm who you are, what you do, and where you serve.

SAFEGUARD 01

WHAT TYPE OF SCHEMA ARE YOU USING?

If they cannot name schema types, they are guessing. You want specifics, not “we add schema.”

Good answers sound like

  • LocalBusiness (or a relevant subtype)
  • Organization + WebSite
  • WebPage
  • Service or Product (depending on the page)
  • FAQPage (when FAQs exist on the page)
  • BreadcrumbList
SAFEGUARD 02

WHERE DOES THE SCHEMA GO?

Real AISEO has placement and scope. Not “it’s in a plugin somewhere.” Not “it’s automatic.”

Correct answers include

  • JSON-LD scripts in the head or near the end of body
  • On the page it describes, not dumped sitewide by default
  • Server-rendered so crawlers reliably see it
  • Validated after deployment
SAFEGUARD 03

WHAT DID YOU CHANGE ON MY WEBSITE?

This is the receipt check. If they cannot show exact changes, they did not do AISEO.

Proof looks like

  • Pages created or rewritten (service pages, location pages, FAQs)
  • Before and after structure improvements (headings, answers, entities)
  • Schema code samples and where they live
  • Internal link changes and why
  • Local trust alignment fixes (NAP, listings, GBP match)
  • Crawlability upgrades (sitemaps, canonicals, index cleanup)

If an agency cannot answer those three questions clearly, they are not selling AISEO. They are selling a rebranded SEO package with a fresh coat of paint.

AISEO DROPZONE QUESTIONS

These are the questions business owners ask when AI answers start showing up, clicks drop, and competitors start getting recommended.

What is AISEO?

AISEO is AI search optimization. It is the practice of making your website machine legible so AI systems can parse your structure, schema, entities, and local facts without guessing.

How is AISEO different from traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO focuses on rankings and blue links. AISEO focuses on citations and recommendations inside AI answers, which means building clean schema, entity clarity, local trust alignment, and quotable answers.

How do I vet an agency selling AISEO?

Ask three questions: what type of schema they are using, where the schema goes, and what they changed on your website. Real AISEO is implementation with receipts, not a buzzword report.

What pages do I need for AI to understand my services?

One strong page per core service, plus supporting answers. If you offer 8 services, do not cram them into one page. Add location clarity where it makes sense and keep the names consistent across your site.

Does AI pull info from my website or my Google Business Profile?

Both, plus other sources. Your GBP is a major trust signal for local intent, but AI often pulls the explanation from websites and other trusted pages. The safest move is alignment: same services, same address, same hours, same story everywhere.

What is schema, and do I really need it?

Schema is a set of labels that tells machines what your business and pages are about. You can rank without it, but AI selection gets harder when machines have to guess.

How long does it take AI to update after I change hours or address?

It varies. Some systems update fast, others lag because they cross-check multiple sources. If old directories still show the wrong info, AI may keep repeating it. AISEO includes consistency work so updates stick.
